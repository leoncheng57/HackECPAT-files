· How You Can Help ·

Volunteer!

  • Hand out promotional materials, such as ECPAT-USA brochures.
  • Wrap soap, typically used in hotels, with the National Human Trafficking Hotline Number (1-888-373-7888) and encourage employees to give them out to guests or perhaps a person who looks like they could be a victim of human trafficking.
  • Visit our website at epcatusa.org to learn more about the issue, find internship and volunteer opportunities!

Take Initiative!

  • Plan a school movie screening for "What I Have Been Through Is Not Who I Am."
  • Bring peers to local organizations that work in the field of anti-human trafficking.
    This may spark interest among them to begin working in this field.
  • Human Trafficking Awareness Day is on January 11th. Wear navy blue to show your support!
  • Plan events, such as inviting speakers to your school.
  • Distribute a petition to your classmates on something you would like to see addressed or changed in the anti-trafficking field.

Spread Awareness by Sharing the ECPAT-USA Website on Social Media!
